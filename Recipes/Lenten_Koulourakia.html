Lenten Koulourakia

Can’t wait till Easter to try these delicious Greek cookies? With this fast-approved version, you don’t have to!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 20 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • ½ c. sugar
  • ½ c. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • ¼ tsp. grated orange peel
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. cloves
  • 2 ¼ c. flour, sifted

Directions

  1. Beat sugar, oil, orange juice and baking powder.
  2. Add orange peel, cinnamon, cloves, and 2 cups flour. Mix well.
  3. Add last ¼ cup of flour as needed, to creat a medium-soft dough.
  4. Roll onto baking sheets. To roll, make balls that are a level tbsp., then roll the balls into a snake. Finally, braid two snakes together to get a completed cookie.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes at 350.
