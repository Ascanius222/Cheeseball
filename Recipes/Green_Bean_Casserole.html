Green Bean Casserole

A staple of holiday spreads everywhere, not least because all of its main ingredients can come from cans, should you so desire.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 box chicken broth
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 diced onion
  • 4 c. green beans
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can French’s onions
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • ½ tsp. black pepper
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder

Directions

  1. Melt butter into skillet. Sauté onions.
  2. Boil green beans in chicken broth for 10 minutes. Drain.
  3. Add green beans, mushroom soup, some French’s onions and seasonings to onions. Stir.
  4. Pour into greased casserole dish. Tope with remaining French’s onions. Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes.
