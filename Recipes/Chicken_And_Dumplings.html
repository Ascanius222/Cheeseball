Chicken and Dumplings

It’s hard to get more down-on-the-farm than this. A country classic that’s not to be missed.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 140 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

    Chicken
  • 3 quarts water
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 onion
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3-6 parsley leaves
  • 1 tsp. ground black pepper
    • Dumplings
  • 2 c. flour
  • 1 Tbsp. baking powder
  • 1 ¼ tsp. salt
  • 1 c + 2 Tbsp. milk

Directions

  1. Boil water in large pot.
  2. Add chicken, 1 tsp. salt onion, celery, garlic, bay leaf, and parsley.
  3. Reduce heat to simmer, and cook uncovered for 2 hours. The liquid will reduce by a third during this time.
  4. Remove chicken, then strain vegetables out of liquid. Discard vegetables.
  5. Pour stock back into pot. Add pepper and ½ tsp. of salt and reheat.
  6. For the dumplings, combine flour baking powder, salt, and milk in medium bowl.
  7. Stir until smooth, then let rest for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Drop by the spoonful into broth. Dumplings should swell, then shrink as they partially dissolve.
  9. Stir for up to 20 minutes, until broth thickens.
  10. Add chicken to individual bowls of broth per serving.
