Welsh Rarebit

Sure it’s not an actual rabbit. But then again you’re probably not actually Welsh either. Wherever you’re from, you're sure to find this bread topped with a cheesy sauce tasty.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 0 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 2 Tbsp. flour
  • 1 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • ½ c porter beer
  • ¾ c. heavy cream
  • 2 drops hot pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ c cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 loaf bread

Directions

  1. Melt butter, and whisk in flour, and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper, and stir until smooth.
  3. Add beer, and then add cream.
  4. Add cheese, then simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Pour mix over bread and serve.
