Peanut Butter Pie

Good-‘ol-boys can’t get enough of this southern classic.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.(plus 1 hour cooling)
  • Cook Time: 5-7 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 25 Oreos
  • 4 Tbsp. melted butter
  • 1 c. creamy peanut butter
  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ¼ c. powdered sugar
  • 8 oz. cool whip, thawed

Directions

  1. Crush Oreos into fine crumbs. Pour in melted butter and combine.
  2. Press mixture into pie pan and bake at 350℉ for 5-7 minutes. Cool completely.
  3. Mix peanut butter with cream cheese until smooth.
  4. Add powdered sugar and beat until smooth.
  5. Add cool whip and beat until smooth and fluffy.
  6. Pour filling into crust and chill at least 1 hour.
