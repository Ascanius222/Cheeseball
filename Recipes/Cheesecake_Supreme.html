Cheesecake Supreme

A decadent classic!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.(with at least 3 hours of chilling)
  • Cook Time: 63 min.
  • Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

    Crust
  • 1 c. flour
  • ¼ c. sugar
  • 1 tsp. lemon peel
  • ½ c. butter
  • 1 beaten egg yolk
  • ¼ tsp. vanilla
    • Filling
  • 40 oz. cream cheese
  • ¾ tsp. lemon peel
  • ¼ tsp. vanilla
  • 1 ¾ c. sugar
  • 3 Tbsp. flour
  • ¼ tsp. salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ c. whipping cream

Directions

  1. For the crust, combine flour, sugar and lemon peel. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  2. Add egg yolk and vanilla. Mix well.
  3. Pat 1/3 of dough on bottom of 9” springform pan with sides removed.
  4. Bake at 400 for 8 minutes. Cool.
  5. Butter pan sides and attach.
  6. Pat remaining dough 1 ¾ in. up.
  7. For the filling, cream the cream cheese.
  8. Add lemon peel and vanilla.
  9. Mix sugar, salt, and flour separately. Add this mixture to the cream cheese mix.
  10. Add eggs and egg yolks one at a time, then add whipping cream.
  11. Bake at 300℉ for 55 minutes.
  12. Cool 30 minutes, and loosen cake from sides of pan with a spatula.
  13. Cool 30 more minutes, then remove pan sides.
  14. Cool for 2 hours, and add topping if desired.
  15. Chill until ready to serve.
