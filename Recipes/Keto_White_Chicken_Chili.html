Keto White Chicken Chili

A delicious keto-friendly chicken chili, featuring green chilies and pepper-jack cheese.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 10-20 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • Olive oil and butter (for sautéing chicken)
  • 1 ½ tsp. chili powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • 6 Tbsp. butter
  • 2 c. heavy cream
  • 4 oz. cream cheese
  • ¾ c. chicken broth
  • 1 ½ Tbsp. onion powder
  • 2 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ c. shredded pepper-jack cheese
  • 8 oz. chopped green chilies

Directions

  1. Heat pressure cooker on sauté setting with olive oil and butter.
  2. Cook chicken breasts with chili powder, salt and pepper. Remove when cooked and shred.
  3. Add butter, cream, cream cheese, chicken broth, and spices to cooker.
  4. Cook until ingredients reach a smooth consistency (about 5 minutes).
  5. Add shredded chicken, cheese, and chilies to cooker.
  6. Cook for 10 minutes (with pressure) or 20 minutes (on simmer).
