Zucchini Appetizers

A crunchy appetizer drawing influence from Greek cuisine.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 25 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 c. thinly sliced zucchini (about 3 small zucchinis or 1 ½ medium)
  • 1 c. Bisquick baking mix
  • ½ c. finely chopped onion
  • ½ c. grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp. parsley
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • ½ tsp. oregano
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Grease 13 x 9 in. pan.
  3. Spread ingredient mix in pan and bake until golden brown, about 25 minutes at 350℉.
  4. Break into pieces when cool. Makes about 28 small or 48 very small pieces.
