Celery Almandine

This sautéed celery and almond side-dish makes for a classy (and low-carb) addition to any Thanksgiving table!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 20 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • ¼ c. chicken stock
  • 4 c. celery, sliced diagonally
  • ½ c. slivered, blanched almonds
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 tsp. onion powder or chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp. white wine
  • 1 tsp. parsley

Directions

  1. Slice celery ¼ in thick segments diagonally.
  2. Sauté almonds in 1 Tbsp. butter over low heat until brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. In another pan, melt butter, add celery, chicken stock, and spices.
  4. Cover and cook until tender, about 12 minutes.
  5. Add wine and almonds, cook 3 more minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
