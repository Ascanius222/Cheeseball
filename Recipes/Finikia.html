Finikia

A Greek cookie similar to melomakarona.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 25-30 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 2 c. vegetable oil
  • 1 c. butter (unsalted)
  • 1 c. orange juice
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. cloves
  • 2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 4-7 c. sifted flour
    • Syrup
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 1 c. honey
  • 1 Â½ c. water
  • 1 c. finely chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Combine 4 c. sifted flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  2. In a separate large bowl, combine oil, melted butter, orange juice, honey, sugar, cloves, cinnamon, and vanilla. Mix these until smooth using your hand.
  3. Gradually add flour mixture to large bowl. Add more flour as needed until consistency is almost gritty.
  4. Form dough into egg-like ovals in lightly greased pans.
  5. Bake 25-30 minutes at 350℉.
  6. Boil syrup ingredients together in a pot for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. While cookies are still warm dip them into the syrup.
  8. Remove and top with walnuts.
