Chocolate Pudding Pie

Chocolate. Pudding. Pie. ‘nough said.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min. (plus 4 hours of cooling)
  • Cook Time: 6-8 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 prepared pie crust
  • 1 ½ c. sugar
  • ¼ c. corn starch
  • ¼ tsp. salt
  • 3 c. milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 6.5 oz. bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp. butter

Directions

  1. Combine sugar, corn starch, and salt in medium pot. Pour in milk and egg yolks and whisk together.
  2. Stir over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat.
  3. Add chocolate, vanilla, and butter, then stir until combined.
  4. Pour into pie crust and chill for 4 hours uncovered.
