Pumpkin Bread

Tired of pumpkin lattes and pumpkin pies? Try pumpkin bread!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min.
  • Cook Time: 60-70 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 c. flour
  • 2 tsp. cinnamon
  • ½ tsp. ground cloves
  • 1.2 tsp. nutmeg
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 2 c. pumpkin
  • 1 ½ c. vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ c. raisins or semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine flour, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, salt, and baking soda. Set aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix sugar, pumpkin, and oil.
  3. Stir in dry ingredients until just moistened. Add chocolate chips or raisins.
  4. Pour into two greased bread loaf pans.
  5. Bake at 350℉ 60-70 minutes (until the loaves pass the toothpick test).
  6. Cool 10 minutes before removing.
