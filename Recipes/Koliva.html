Koliva

In the Greek Orthodox Church, this granola-like mixture of boiled farro, nuts, and spices is made for commemorations of the departed.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 40 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 c. farro (about ½ a bag)
  • 1 c. panko (about ½ a container)
  • 1 c. finely chopped walnuts (about ½ a bag)
  • ½ c. golden raisins
  • ½ c. raisins
  • 1-2 Tbsp. honey
  • ¼ c. parsley
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. pumpkin spice
  • Powdered sugar (for topping)
  • Slivered almonds (for topping design)

Directions

  1. Rinse farro, then boil (like pasta) for 30 min.
  2. Drain and spread farro on parchment to dry.
  3. Toast panko and walnuts.
  4. Place dry farro in mixing bowl. Add honey and mix.
  5. Add spices, walnuts, and panko. Mix.
  6. Rinse stickiness from raisins and pat them dry. Add raisins to mix.
  7. Pour mix into serving bowl. Sift powdered sugar on top.
  8. Use slivered almonds to create cross design on top of powdered sugar.
