Savory Onion Pie

A recipe based on a colonial original. “It tastes better than it sounds!” – Ben Franklin, probably.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 45 min.
  • Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

    Crust
  • 2 c. flour
  • 12 Tbsp. butter, chilled
  • 3-5 Tbsp. cold water
    • Filling
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • 2-3 apples
  • 2-4 onions
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  • ½ tsp. nutmeg
  • Dash of mace

Directions

  1. For crust, chop butter into pieces and combine with flower and water.
  2. Form crust into pie tin, placing pats of beneath the crust.
  3. For the filling, slice potatoes, apples, onions, and eggs into 1/8-1/4 in. thick slices.
  4. Mix the salt and spices together separately.
  5. Layer sliced potatoes into pie, then sprinkle on the spice mix.
  6. Repeat the process with apples, then spice, onion, then spice, eggs, then spice, apples, etc.
  7. Top off filling with pats of butter.
  8. Place on top crust and pinch together. Place three slices in top to vent.
  9. Bake at 350℉ for 45 minutes.
