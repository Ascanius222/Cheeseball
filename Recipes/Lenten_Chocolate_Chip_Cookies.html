Lenten Chocolate Chip Cookies

An American Classic, modified to be fast-approved.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 8-10 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ c. margarine
  • 1 c. brown sugar
  • 1 c. sugar
  • ½ c. orange juice
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 4 ½ c. flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 16 oz. Ghirardelli chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda in bowl.
  2. Mix margarine, sugar and brown sugar. Add orange juice, mix well, and then add vanilla.
  3. Mix all ingredients. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Bake 8-10 minutes at 350℉ on ungreased cookie sheets.
