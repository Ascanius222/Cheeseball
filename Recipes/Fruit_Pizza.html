Fruit Pizza

When you put fruit on a pizza, you can have pizza anytime (or at least for dessert!). Fresh fruit and sugar cookie ‘crust’ make a flavor combination as delicious as it is unexpected.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 5 min.
  • Cook Time: 13-18 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

    Crust
  • ¾ c. water
  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • 2 c. flour
  • ¼ c. milk
    • Glaze
  • ¼ c. sugar
  • ¾ c. pineapple juice
  • 1 Tbps. corn starch
    • Toppings
  • Pineapple
  • Banana
  • Orange
  • Lemon juice

Directions

  1. For the crust, mix sugar and butter, then add flour and milk.
  2. Press dough onto pizza pan, and bake 13-18 minutes at 400 ℉.
  3. For glaze, mix sugar and cornstarch in a saucepan.
  4. Add pineapple juice and bring to boil.
  5. Boil 1 minute, then cool for 10 minutes.
  6. Place fruit on crust, then glaze, then drizzle on lemon juice.
