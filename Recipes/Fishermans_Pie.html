Fisherman's 'Pie'

This Keto-friendly recipe uses an almond flour crust filled with fish and veggies. A few tweaks let you lose the crust and turn the filling into a hearty stew.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 25 min.
  • Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

    Crust
  • 1 ½ c. almond flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 2 Tbsp. oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbsp. water
    • Filling
  • 1 ¾ c. milk
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 lb. white fish
  • 3 Tbsp. butter
  • 3 ½ Tbsp. tapioca flour
  • 8 oz. peas
  • 8 oz. carrots, diced
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. For crust, combine almond flour, salt and baking soda.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine oil, egg and water.
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Form dough into pie pan, and bake 8-10 min. at 350.
  5. Cool before filling.
  6. (Double the above process to make a top pie crust, or exclude it to make a fish stew.)
  7. For filling/stew, pour milk into lidded saucepan. Add onion, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  8. Add fish and bring to simmering. Cook 2 minutes.
  9. Remove pan from heat and leave to infuse for 20 minutes.
  10. Drain fish through colander and retain milk.
  11. Melt butter and stir in tapioca, cooking for a few seconds before adding in the milk.
  12. Simmer on medium heat 3-4 minutes, stirring constantly until sauce is smooth.
  13. Add peas and carrots and stir again.
  14. Divide fish into chunks and add into sauce, pour into crust.
  15. Bake at 400 for 25 minutes (or cook in pot for same time if making stew).
