Cheesy Broccoli-Chicken Casserole

A warm and hearty chicken broccoli casserole, oozing with cheesy goodness. Perfect for cold winter nights, and the ultimate in Midwestern comfort foods!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 steamed bag of broccoli
  • 6 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 ¾ c. heavy cream
  • 1 bag shredded cheese
  • 1 Tbsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • Butter and olive oil, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ℉.
  2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Melt oil and butter in large pan.
  3. Cook chicken with salt and pepper. Steam broccoli simultaneously.
  4. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan, reserving oil and butter. Add cream, garlic powder, and ½ Tbsp. of Italian seasoning. Then add cream cheese and stir until smooth.
  5. Reserve ¼ cup of shredded cheese, and add the remaining portion to the sauce.
  6. Grease a casserole dish, then add chicken, broccoli, and cheese sauce.
  7. Top casserole with remaining shredded cheese and seasoning.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes.
