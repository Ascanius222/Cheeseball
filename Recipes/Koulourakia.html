Koulourakia

A delightful butter cookie traditionally served during the Easter season in Greece.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 15 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 1 ½ c. sweet butter
  • 1 ½ c. sugar
  • 3 tsp. vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 c. all-purpose flour
  • 3 tsp. baking powder
  • ½ tsp. baking soda
  • 1 ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • 3 Tbsp. orange juice
    • Glaze
  • 1 egg
  • Butter
  • 2 Tbsp. milk

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  2. Add eggs, vanilla, and orange juice. Beat well to combine.
  3. Combine all dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  5. Roll out the dough and place on parchment.
  6. Beat glaze ingredients together, then brush dough with glaze.
  7. To roll, make balls that are a level tbsp., then roll the balls into a snake. Finally, braid two snakes together to get a completed cookie.
  8. Bake for 14 minutes at 350℉.
