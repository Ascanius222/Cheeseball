Carrot Cake

“If rabbits had hands and could bake, then they would bake their cakes as carrot cakes.” –Xenophanes

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 25-50 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

    Cake
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 1 c. vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 c.flour
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 2 c. grated carrots
    • Glaze
  • 1 stick butter, softened
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 lb. powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. pecans, chopped finely

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients (flour, salt baking soda and powder, cinnamon), and set aside.
  2. Mix butter, sugar and eggs in a separate bowl. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  3. Add carrots and mix well.
  4. Bake in a greased 9x13 in. pan or Bundt at 350℉ for 25 minutes (9x13) or 50 minutes (Bundt).
  5. For the glaze, cream butter and cream cheese together.
  6. Add sugar and vanilla. Mix in nuts if desired. Spread on cooled cake.
