Keto Pumpkin Pie

Based on Squanto’s secret recipe.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min.
  • Cook Time: 50-60 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

    Crust
  • 2 ¼ c. almond flour
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 4 tsp. sweetener
  • 3 Tbsp. butter, softened
  • 1 egg
    • Filling
  • 2 c. pumpkin puree
  • 1 c. heavy cream
  • ¾ c. sweetener
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 Tbsp. pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs

Directions

  1. Mix all dough ingredients together.
  2. Press dough into 9 in pie pan, and bake about 10 minutes at 350, until golden brown.
  3. For the filling, beat eggs with mixer.
  4. Add pumpkin, vanilla, sweetener, and spices. Mix well.
  5. Add cream until combined fully.
  6. Pour into pie crust and bake 40-50 minutes, or until it passes the toothpick test.
  7. Cool completely before serving.
