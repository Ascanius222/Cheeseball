Deviled Eggs

Eggs!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 14 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • Â½ c. mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp. white vinegar
  • 2 tsp. mustard
  • 1/8 tsp. black pepper
  • Paprika (to taste)

Directions

  1. Place eggs in pot and cover with water (to 1 in. above eggs).
  2. Bring to boil.
  3. Remove heat, cover pot, and let stand for 14 minutes.
  4. Peel eggs and cut in half lengthwise.
  5. Transfer yolks to bowl and mash with mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, and pepper.
  6. Return deviled mixture to egg whites, and garnish with paprika.
