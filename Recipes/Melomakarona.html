Melomakarona

A honey-drenched cookie that’s a Christmas staple in Greece!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 25-30 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 1 c. orange juice
  • ¾ c. sugar
  • 2 c. olive oil
  • 6 ½ c. flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
    • Syrup
  • 1 c. honey
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 1 ½ c. water
  • 3-4 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • Chopped walnuts (for topping)

Directions

  1. Combine sugar and olive oil, then mix, and add in orange juice.
  2. Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine all the dough ingredients slowly (cup by cup) until mixed.
  4. Place dough on parchment lined baking sheets.
  5. Bake at 350 for 25-30 min.
  6. Add all syrup ingredients into a pot.
  7. Bring to boil, then simmer for 10-15 minutes, creating a syrup.
  8. Dunk hot cookies in the cool syrup. Top with walnuts if desired.
