Tomato Cream Sauce

A pasta sauce that you can brag about making yourself, and still tasty enough that your friends will eat it

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 30 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 c. water (from pasta)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • 1 c. heavy cream
  • Basil (to taste)
  • Parmesan cheese (to taste)

Directions

  1. Warm butter and oil over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic for one minute.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and sugar.
  4. Cook over low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in cream.
  6. Add cheese, thinning sauce with pasta water if needed.
  7. Add basil and pour sauce onto pasta.
