Meatza

A pizza with a meat crust? Is it a keto-friendly Italian treat or an abomination against the Lord? You be the judge!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 5 min.
  • Cook Time: 20-25 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • ½ bag shredded cheese
  • ½ jar tomato sauce (Classico Traditional works well)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic power, and Italian seasoning (to taste)
  • Toppings of choice

Directions

  1. Pour ground beef into bowl, add egg and seasonings, and mix well with a fork.
  2. Press ground beef mixture into metal pie pan, mimicking the shape of a pie crust.
  3. Bake 10-15 minutes at 400℉.

Tags: Beef; Entrée; Keto
