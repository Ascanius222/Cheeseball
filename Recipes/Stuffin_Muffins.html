Stuffin’ Muffins

A mouth-watering stuffing baked in muffin tins, so that it’s crispy all the way through. Once you try it, you’ll never want to eat regular stuffing again! Can be made carb-friendly!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 45 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tube mild breakfast sausage
  • 1 loaf of bread, torn and staled (cauliflower can be sautéed and used a sub)
  • 5 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 1 small package of julienned carrots
  • 1 lb. mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ c. parsley
  • 2 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 c. chicken broth

Directions

  1. Cook sausage in large skillet. Remove.
  2. Sauté mushrooms in sausage grease. Remove, then sauté onions and garlic, then celery and carrots. Supplement sausage grease with olive oil if needed.
  3. Add everything to bread in large bowl.
  4. In separate bowl, beat eggs with chicken broth. Pour this binding mixture into main bowl.
  5. Mix by hand until ingredients and moist.
  6. Pack into greased cupcake trays.
  7. Bake at 350℉ for 30 minutes, until golden brown and crispy.
