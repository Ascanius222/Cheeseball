Rosemary and Red Wine Beef Stew

A lovely and savory beef stew. Keto friendly!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. stewing beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 c. beef broth
  • 2 c. water
  • 1 ½ c. dry red wine
  • 16 oz. frozen carrots (1 bag)
  • 16 oz. frozen green beans (1 bag)
  • Butter
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • White pepper (to taste)
  • Thyme (to taste)
  • Bay leaves (1-2)
  • Rosemary (to taste)
  • Oregano (to taste)
  • Parsley (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pat beef dry between two paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Brown beef in butter on medium heat (works best in two batches).
  3. Remove beef, and sauté onions and garlic in pot.
  4. Add broth, wine, and water. Season to taste with herbs and spices.
  5. Add beef and vegetables.
  6. Cook on high until done.
