Low-Carb Chocolate Chip Cookies

Someday, the reckless water management of California almond growers will turn their land into a desert and the price of almonds will skyrocket. But until then, you can enjoy these delicious chocolate chip cookies—made with keto-friendly almond flour!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 10-12 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ c almond flour
  • 3 Tbsp. coconut flour
  • ½ tsp. baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ c. butter
  • ¼ c. Truvia
  • 1/3 c. chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Mix almond and coconut flours with baking soda and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix vanilla, egg, butter, and Truvia.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Add chocolate chips.
  5. Bake 10-12 minutes at 350℉.
