Gingerbread

“Was he made of house, or his house made of flesh? The gingerbread man screamed, for he did not know.”

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 2 min. (plus overnight dough resting
  • Cook Time: 8-12 min.
  • Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

    Gingerbread
  • 6 c. flour
  • ½ tsp. baking powder
  • 4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp. ground cloves or allspice
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 12 Tbsp. butter, softened
  • 1 ½ c. brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 c. molasses
  • 1 Tbsp. water
    • Glaze
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 2/3 c. powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Mix butter and brown sugar in a second bowl.
  3. Add eggs, molasses, and water to butter and brown sugar.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Wrap in saran wrap and refrigerate overnight.
  6. The next day, let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes before rolling out.
  7. Roll to about ¼ in thickness and cut desired shapes.
  8. Bake at 350℉ for 8-12 minutes.
  9. For the glaze, mix egg whites and 1 1/3 c powdered sugar.
  10. Add the remaining powdered sugar until peaks form.
  11. Glaze cookies while warm.
