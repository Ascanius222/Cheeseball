Mighty Meaty Meatloaf

Did you know most meatloaf recipes call for bread crumbs and so are not keto friendly? Not so for this mighty champion of meat!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min.
  • Cook Time: 70 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 4 oz. ketchup
  • 1 ½ Tbsp. soy sauce
  • ½ Tbsp. onion powder
  • ½ Tbsp. parsley
  • ½ Tbsp. garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ c. parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Spread in brad loaf pan and bake at 350℉ for 70 minutes, until internal temperature is 110.
  3. Drain fat if needed and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
