Apple Pie

Can’t have a summer without baseball, and you can’t have a recipe book without apple pie.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 60 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • ½ c. butter
  • 3 Tbsp. flour
  • ¼ c. water
  • ½ c. sugar
  • ½ c. brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples
  • 2 prepared pie crusts

Directions

  1. Peel, core, and slice apples.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan, and stir in flour to form paste.
  3. Add water and sugar and bring to boil.
  4. Place bottom crust in a pan, and fill with apples, mounded slightly.
  5. Divide other crust into strips, and form a lattice over the apples.
  6. Pour caramel mix over pie slowly.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes at 425℉, then 45 minutes at 350℉, until apples are soft.
