Keto Thin Mints

So good the Girl Scouts want the recipe!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 7 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 2 Tbsp. cocoa powder
  • 1 c. almond flour
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp. peppermint extract
  • Stevia in 3 Tbsp. water
    • Coating
  • ½ c. chocolate chips
  • ¼ tsp. peppermint extract
  • 2 tsp. oil

Directions

  1. Combine dough ingredients in bowl and mix.
  2. Shape dough into small circles on parchment-lined baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 7 minutes at 350, then set in fridge to cool.
  4. For the coating, melt the chocolate and oil together (power level 5 in the microwave at 30 sec increments works best). Add peppermint extract to melted ingredients.
  5. Dip cookies in coating, then place in freezer to cool solid.
