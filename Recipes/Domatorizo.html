Domatorizo

A Greek tomato-rice that makes a perfect accompaniment to lamb or chicken.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 15 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 4 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 c. whole tomatoes, canned
  • 1 c. long grain rice
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Â¼ tsp. pepper

Directions

  1. Cook onion in oil until soft.
  2. Drain tomatoes, reserving liquid.
  3. Add tomatoes to onions, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add rice, stirring over medium heat until lightly brown.
  5. Add enough water to reserved tomato juice to make 2 cups. Add liquid to pot.
  6. Cover and bring to boil. Lower heat, cook 15 minutes. The liquid should be absorbed when done.
