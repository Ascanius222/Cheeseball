Beef and Broccoli

A simple yet delicious Chinese dish.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 10 min. (plus an additional 20-60 minutes for marinating)
  • Cook Time: 20 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1/3 c. soy sauce
  • ½ tsp. sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. garlic
  • 1 tsp. ginger
  • 1 tsp. thickener
  • 1 tsp. water
  • 1 lb. beef
  • 24-32 oz. broccoli (1-2 bags)
  • 3 tsp. vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, garlic and ginger in a bowl.
  2. Reserve half of mixture and use other half to marinade beef (20-60 minutes).
  3. Just before stir frying, add thickener and water to second half of mixture, making a sauce.
  4. Use oil to stir fry beef.
  5. Remove beef and stir fry broccoli.
  6. Add beef again and make a well in center of ingredients.
  7. Pour sauce into this well. Cook until thickened (about 2 minutes).
