Kourambiedes

Melt-in-your-mouth almond butter cookies that are a Christmas staple in Greece!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 20 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. unsalted butter
  • ½ c. powdered sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 jigger whiskey
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • ½ tsp. almond extract
  • 6 Tbsp. toasted almonds, chopped fine
  • 4 c. sifted flour
  • 3 c. sifted powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Beat butter and sugar until creamy.
  2. Add egg yolk, flavorings, and almonds. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add flour to make soft dough.
  4. Roll into balls and place on cookie sheet.
  5. Bake at 350℉ for 20 minutes.
  6. Dust surface, place cookies, and sift sugar on them while warm.
