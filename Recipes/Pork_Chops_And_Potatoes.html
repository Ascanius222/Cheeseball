Pork Chops and Potatoes

A stick-to-your-ribs country classic beloved by all (except for pigs!).

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 family sized can of Cream of Mushroom soup (Campbell’s Fat-Free works best)
  • 1 soup can water
  • 6 potatoes
  • 4 pork chops
  • 1-2 Tbsp. butter
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pierce potatoes with fork and place in microwave for 5 minutes. Turn over potatoes and microwave for another 5 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in large skillet. Brown pork chops.
  3. Season chops with salt and pepper, then cook till almost done. Remove chops.
  4. Peel cooked potatoes and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Remove fat from chops.
  6. Add mushroom soup to pan. Stir with metal spoon to scrape up flavorings.
  7. Add can of water and stir.
  8. Heat to simmer, add potatoes and cook 15 minutes.
  9. Add chops and cook an additional 15 minutes.
