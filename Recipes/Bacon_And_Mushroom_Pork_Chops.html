Bacon and Mushroom Pork Chops

Pork chops simmered in a creamy mushroom sauce. A hearty meal perfect for fall or winter dinners.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 5 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 6 strips of bacon
  • 3-4 pork chops
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 8 oz. mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • Thyme (to taste)
  • Olive oil or butter (for sautéing)
  • 2 Tbsp. flour (or xanthan gum)
  • 2/3 c. chicken broth
  • ½ c. heavy cream

Directions

  1. Fry bacon in large pan until crispy. Remove and break into pieces.
  2. Season chops with salt and pepper. Cook in pan about 4 minutes on each side, then remove.
  3. Turn heat to medium, and add mushrooms, thyme, and garlic. Cook about 5 minutes.
  4. Add flour or xanthan gum and chicken broth. Stir to a boil, creating a slurry.
  5. Add heavy cream while still boiling and stir until smooth. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Add back in chops and bacon. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
