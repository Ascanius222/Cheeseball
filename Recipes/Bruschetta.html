Bruschetta

Fresh tomatoes, basil, and cheese on toasted bread. Yum!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 0 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • ¼ c. olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp. chopped basil
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 4 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp. grated parmesan
  • 1 loaf French bread

Directions

  1. Combine olive oil, basil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add tomatoes to mixture.
  3. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top, then refrigerate for 1 hour.
  4. Slice and toast French bread.
  5. Serve bruschetta on toasted bread while bread is still warm.
