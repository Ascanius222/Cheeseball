Black Bean Soup

A quick and delicious vegetarian soup!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 10 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 ½ c. water
  • 2 15 oz. cans black beans, drained and washed
  • 1 tsp. sea salt
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 1 Tbsp. cilantro
  • Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Chop onions, garlic, carrots, and pepper.
  2. In a large soup pot on medium-high heat, sauté chopped vegetables for 10 min.
  3. Add water and ½ can of beans. Blend.
  4. Return to medium-high heat. Add remaining beans and spices.
