Lenten Chocolate Cake

Chocolate may be the devil’s food, but this fast-approved cake won’t send you to meet him if you eat it during Lent.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 45 min. (or 20 min. for cupcake)
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 c. flour
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 6 Tbsp. cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 3 Tbsp. white vinegar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • ¾ c. vegetable oil
  • 2 c. cold water
  • 1 c. nuts (optional)
  • 1 c. confectioner’s sugar

Directions

  1. Sift flour, baking soda, cocoa powder, and salt into a mixing bowl and whisk.
  2. Combine sugar vinegar vanilla extract, vegetable oil, and water. Whisk together until sugar is dissolved and wet ingredients are combined.
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Mix in nuts if desired.
  4. Bake at 350 in 9x13 in. greased pan for 45 minutes (or bake as 24 cupcakes for 20 minutes).
  5. Powder with confectioner’s sugar and serve.
