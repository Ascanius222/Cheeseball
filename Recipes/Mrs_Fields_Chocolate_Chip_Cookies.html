Mrs. Field’s Chocolate Chip Cookies

Who’s Mrs. Fields? Who cares! All that matters is that these cookies are amazing!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 20 min.
  • Cook Time: 10 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 c. butter
  • 2 c. brown sugar
  • 1 ½ c. granulated white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 5 c. oatmeal
  • 4 c. flour
  • 24 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix butter, brown sugar, sugar, eggs and vanilla in large mixing bowl.
  2. Grind oatmeal in blender into a fine powder.
  3. Mix oatmeal powder with flour, and add to main ingredient bowl. Stir well.
  4. Add chocolate chips and nuts.
  5. Drop onto cookie sheet.
  6. Bake 8-10 minutes at 375℉.
