Ham-Bean Soup

A staple of Midwestern winter-time cooking. Consider eating on New Year’s for good luck!

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 20 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 medium carrots
  • 2 celery ribs
  • ½ c. chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 4 cans Great Northern beans
  • 4 c. chicken broth
  • 2 c. chopped ham
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • ½ tsp. minced garlic
  • ¼ tsp. pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Directions

  1. Drain and rinse beans.
  2. Chop carrots and celery, then sauté them with the onion until tender.
  3. Add all the ingredients to a stew pot. Bring to boil, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Discard bay leaf.
