Chicken Noodle Soup

Nothing says ‘comfort food’ or ‘convalescence’ like chicken noodle soup.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 15 min.
  • Cook Time: 30 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 c. water
  • 1 box chicken broth
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 onion
  • ½ bag egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Boil broth and water.
  3. Add vegetables and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add noodles, salt, and pepper, and cook for 10 minutes.
