Gram-Gram's Pumpkin Pie

An old family recipe for a holiday classic. Makes two pies.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 30 min.
  • Cook Time: 70 min.
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • ¼ tsp. ginger
  • 1 c. milk
  • 2 eggs (slightly beaten)
  • 1 ¼ c. canned pumpkin
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp. nutmeg
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ¼ tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. cream
  • 2 pre-made pie crusts (store or homemade)

Directions

  1. Mix sugar, spices, and salt, then add eggs.
  2. Stir in vanilla and pumpkin.
  3. Heat milk and cream to scalding, then add to pie mixture and stir.
  4. Pour into pie crusts.
  5. Bake 10 minutes at 425℉, then 60 minutes at 325℉, until pies are firm.
