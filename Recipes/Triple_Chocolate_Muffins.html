Triple Chocolate Muffins

The best breakfast (or brunch, or snack) for chocolate lovers.

At A Glance

  • Prep Time: 25 min.
  • Cook Time: 23 min.
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 4 oz. bar of semi-sweet chocolate
  • ½ c. unsalted butter
  • ¾ c. sugar
  • 2 eggs (at room temperature)
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/3 c. sour cream
  • ½ c. flour
  • 1/3 c. unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp. baking soda
  • ¼ tsp. salt
  • 1 c. (heaping) dark chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate bar and butter, whisking until smooth.
  2. Add sugar, eggs, vanilla, and sour cream.
  3. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt, folding in each separately.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Fill muffin liners to top with batter.
  6. Back for 5 minutes at 425, then 17 minutes at 350.
